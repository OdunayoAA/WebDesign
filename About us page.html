We are a group of well developed farmers, in north holland with a lot of quality products that could be used by good people but instead go to waste because they are not sold quickly. For this reason we come together as a group to create a space where this products can be sold very easily. Where else can this be done if not on the most used platform, we create a website where people can see our products and place an order by a simple call and whatever they want will be delivered right at their door steps.
We are located at Haagse Markt Den Haag, we currently deliver around The Hague and delft region but in the coming months we hope to expand to other regions and keep people fed and reduce waste at the same time.
